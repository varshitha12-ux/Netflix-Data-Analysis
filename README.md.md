**# Netflix Data Analysis**



**## Project Overview**



This project analyzes Netflix Movies and TV Shows dataset from Kaggle using Python.



The objective is to understand:



\- Distribution of Movies and TV Shows

\- Most popular genres

\- Top content producing countries

\- Content growth over years



\---



**## Tools Used**



\- Python

\- Pandas

\- Matplotlib

\- Jupyter Notebook

\- Data Cleaning

\- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)



\---



**## Dataset**



Netflix Movies and TV Shows Dataset from Kaggle.



Dataset contains:



\- Title

\- Type

\- Country

\- Genre

\- Release Year

\- Duration



\---



**## Questions Solved**



1\. How many Movies and TV Shows are available?

2\. What are the most common genres?

3\. Which countries produce the most Netflix content?

4\. How has Netflix content grown over the years?

**## Key Insights**



\- Movies dominate Netflix's content library compared to TV Shows.

\- The United States is the leading producer of Netflix content.

\- Drama and International Movies are among the most popular genres.

\- Netflix experienced rapid content growth after 2015.
